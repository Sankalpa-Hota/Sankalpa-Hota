Welcome to my blog

Feb 8, 2025

I'm starting this space to share notes on what I'm working on and thinking about: VLSI, RTL design, ML accelerators, and the occasional startup or building tangent.

Expect posts on things like:

  • RTL and verification (SystemVerilog, UVM)
  • VLSI and computer architecture
  • ML accelerators (systolic arrays, attention, etc.)
  • Protocols and interfaces (PCIe, NVMe)
  • Building in public and startup ideas

I'll keep posts practical where I can—code snippets, block diagrams, and lessons from debugging and tape-out. Some will be shorter updates; others might be longer deep-dives when a topic deserves it.

If any of that sounds interesting, you can email me or find me on LinkedIn.
